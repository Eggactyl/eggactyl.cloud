## What software can I install with Eggactyl?
- Minecraft Java:
    - *[Vanilla](https://www.minecraft.net/), [Paper](https://papermc.io), [Purpur](https://purpurmc.org), [Magma](https://magmafoundation.org), [Spigot](https://www.spigotmc.org), [Forge](https://files.minecraftforge.net/net/minecraftforge/forge/), [Fabric](https://fabricmc.net), [Cuberite](https://cuberite.org)*
- Minecraft Bedrock:
    - *[Vanilla](https://www.minecraft.net/), [PocketMine-MP](https://pmmp.io)*
- Minecraft Proxies:
    - *[BungeeCord](https://www.spigotmc.org/wiki/bungeecord/), [WaterDog](https://waterdog.dev), [Waterfall](https://papermc.io/software/waterfall), [Velocity](https://papermc.io/software/velocity)*
- Discord Bots:
    - *NodeJS, Python, PHP*
- Voice & Chat Servers:
    - *[TeamSpeak3](https://www.teamspeak.com/), [Lavalink](https://lavalink.dev)*
- GTA:
    - *[SA\:MP](https://sa-mp.com)*
- Steam:
    - *Insurgency, Terraria*

## I got a message saying I was blacklisted for "Automatic Free Trial"
Eggactyl has a system to detect hosts that have free trials and blacklists them.

If you would like to whitelist your server, you can contact support at our [Discord Server](https://discord.eggactyl.cloud)
